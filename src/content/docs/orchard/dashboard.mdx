The dashboard is Orchard's home screen — the single view where the state of your
mint comes together. Instead of querying the mint and reading logs by hand, you
see keys, balances, and mint info at a glance.

## What it surfaces

- **Balances** — what the mint holds and what's outstanding in ecash.
- **Keys** — the active keysets your mint issues against.
- **Mint info** — the public metadata wallets read when they connect.

<Aside type="tip" title="Tend, don't babysit">
  The point of the dashboard is to make the healthy case boring: a quick look
  tells you the mint is fine, and anything that needs attention stands out.
</Aside>
