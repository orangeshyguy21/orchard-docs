Your Lightning node is where the mint actually settles value, and where the bitcoin backing your ecash is custodied.
Connecting it gives you a view of the node and feeds the assets side of your balance sheet.

## Lightning Dashboard

Today, the [home page](/orchard/home/#lightning) summarizes your Lightning node.
A dedicated dashboard is coming soon.

## Mint Assets

Your mint's [balance sheet](/orchard/mint/#dashboard) weighs its liabilities (the ecash in
circulation) against the real bitcoin that backs it (your outbound lightning liquidity).
A connected Lightning node fills in the assets side of the sheet, and lets Orchard
track how your coverage shifts over time.

## AI

Connecting a Lightning node also gives the [AI agent](/orchard/settings/#ai) read-only tools
for knowing what's happening on your node:

- **Lightning Info** — node identity, sync status, and channel counts.
- **Channels** — open and closed channels with balances and peer info.
- **Peers** — connected peers and traffic.
- **Lightning Analytics** — payments, routing, and balance trends over time.
