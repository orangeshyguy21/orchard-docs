Once Orchard is installed and pointed at your mint, this is where you live. Orchard
is the **groundskeeper** for your mint — it surfaces keys, balances, and health in
one place so you can tend the mint instead of babysitting a terminal.

<Aside type="note" title="Not set up yet?">
  These pages assume Orchard is already running against your mint. If you haven't
  reached that point, [install Orchard and connect your services](/install/), or
  build the whole stack from scratch with the [New Mint](/new-mint/) walkthrough.
</Aside>
