## Invites

Users are invited to join your Orchard with expring invite codes you can share with them.
They can be set to expire after a certain amount of time, or you can turn off expiry.
From there they can create their own user account and sign in to your Orchard.

Your invites are scoped to the role **Manager** or **Reader**.

<Aside type="note" title="Publicly available">
  Orchard needs to be publicly available before
  anyone else can sign up.
</Aside>

## Roles

Every member holds one role that decides what they can do:

- **Admin** — full control of the instance. There can only be one admin. This is the account you created at setup.
- **Manager** — can manage the mint.
- **Reader** — can only view the application. No management capabilities.

## Crew Management

Invites and Crew members can be managed from the Crew page.
You can extend or revoke invites,
and you can change a member's role or switch them between Active and Inactive at any time.
