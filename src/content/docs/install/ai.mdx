Orchard includes an **AI agent** — an agent harness that watches your services and
surfaces what needs attention, so you don't have to live in the logs. It reads the
same health and activity Orchard already tracks and turns it into plain answers
about how your mint is doing.

## What it does

- **Watches your stack** — Bitcoin, Lightning, the mint, and the machine they run
  on — for trouble worth your attention.
- **Answers in plain language** instead of raw logs and metrics.

<Aside type="note" title="Optional">
  The AI agent is an optional layer on top of Orchard's monitoring. Your mint runs
  fine without it.
</Aside>

<Aside type="caution" title="Setup steps are on the way">
  Orchard is under active development, and the steps for configuring the AI agent
  are still being written — they land here. The
  [Orchard repository](https://github.com/cashubtc/orchard) has the source code in
  the meantime.
</Aside>
