[Taproot Assets](https://github.com/lightninglabs/taproot-assets) are assets
issued on Bitcoin and transferable over Lightning. If your mint issues ecash
backed by them, Orchard manages that configuration alongside the rest of your
stack, so assets and sats live under one dashboard.

## What to have ready

- **A Lightning backend that speaks Taproot Assets**, connected to your mint and
  reachable from Orchard.
- **The asset details** your mint issues against.

<Aside type="note" title="Optional">
  Taproot Assets are an optional addition. If your mint only issues
  sats-denominated ecash, you can skip this step.
</Aside>

<Aside type="caution" title="Configuration steps are on the way">
  Orchard is under active development, and the steps for configuring Taproot Assets
  are still being written — they land here. The
  [Orchard repository](https://github.com/cashubtc/orchard) has the source code in
  the meantime.
</Aside>
