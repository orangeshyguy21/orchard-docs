The settlement layer. Your Cashu mint issues ecash backed by Lightning, so this
node is what moves real sats in and out when users mint and melt. It anchors to
the Bitcoin node you set up in the previous step.

## Pick an implementation

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="LND">
    [LND](https://github.com/lightningnetwork/lnd) — Lightning Labs' widely
    deployed node. Connects to your mint via gRPC/REST with a macaroon and TLS
    certificate.
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Core Lightning">
    [Core Lightning](https://github.com/ElementsProject/lightning) (CLN) —
    Blockstream's implementation, extensible through plugins and connected over its
    RPC socket.
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## What you'll set up

- **The node**, pointed at your Bitcoin Core node's RPC and notification
  endpoints.
- **A funded channel or two** — outbound liquidity to melt, inbound to mint.
- **API access** your mint can authenticate against in the next step.

<Aside type="caution" title="Exact commands are on the way">
  Until the step-by-step setup lands here, follow the official docs for your chosen
  node — [LND](https://docs.lightning.engineering/) or
  [Core Lightning](https://docs.corelightning.org/) — as the source of truth.
</Aside>
